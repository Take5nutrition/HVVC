What's Included

Season Fees

Season Fees Cover

  • Tournament registration fees
  • Power League Series participation
  • Friendship tournaments
  • Regional competition fees
  • Monthly coaches stipend
  • Practice facility fees

Full Uniform Package

  • 2 game jerseys
  • 2 practice shirts
  • 1 Nike Pro Spandex
  • 1 pair Nike knee pads
  • 1 HVVC sweatshirt
  • 1 team ball bag

Payment Structure

  • Initial deposit upon team selection
  • Monthly installments (due by 5th)
  • Cash, check, or online payment
  • Late fee: $25 after 5 days
Travel/lodging for overnight tournaments is optional and communicated in advance.
How to Pay

Payment Details

01

Deposit

Initial deposit is due upon team selection after tryouts to secure your athlete's spot.

02

Monthly Payments

Remaining balance divided into monthly installments, due by the 5th of each month.

03

Payment Methods

Online payment platform, cash, or check. Mail checks with prior arrangements.

04

Questions?

Reach out to us at take5athletics@gmail.com for any payment questions.

Financial Aid

Scholarship Opportunities

We believe finances should never be a barrier to playing the sport you love.

HVVC Scholarship Fund

  • Based on financial need
  • Application opens October 15
  • Deadline: November 30 at 9 PM
  • Notifications by December 17
  • Distributed in January
  • Spread across remaining payments
Cannot be used for tryout fees, CEVA memberships, or initial club deposits.

CEVA Athlete Success Fund (CASF)

  • External scholarship through CEVA
  • Columbia Empire Volleyball Association
  • Applications open October 1
  • Open to all eligible athletes

Contribute to Scholarships

  • HVVC is a registered 501(c)(3)
  • Tax-deductible donation receipts
  • Contact us to arrange check payments

Need Help with Costs?

HVVC is a registered 501(c)(3) organization. Don't let finances stop your athlete from playing - reach out and we'll work with your family to find a path forward.
