What It Is

The Next Step in Their Journey

HVVC Academy is created for young athletes who have demonstrated strong interest, potential, and readiness for the next step in their volleyball journey. This isn't just a class — it's a stepping stone into the competitive volleyball world.

Athletes train together consistently as a team, learning not only volleyball skills, but also how to practice, communicate, and grow within a team setting.

Get Started
What Athletes Get

Built to Develop the Whole Player

Core Skill Development

Athletes build core volleyball skills while improving consistency, confidence, and overall game awareness — from passing and serving to setting and attacking.

Competitive Game Play

Each session incorporates structured, competitive play — allowing athletes to apply skills in game-like situations while building decision-making and court awareness.

Team Culture & Communication

Athletes learn how to practice with intention, communicate on the court, and grow within a team-first environment — skills that carry far beyond volleyball.

Club-Ready Preparation

This program serves as a direct introduction to club volleyball — preparing athletes for the expectations, structure, and competitive environment of club play in the upcoming fall season.

Positive Environment

Every session is run in a positive, team-first environment where athletes are encouraged to compete, make mistakes, and grow without fear of failure.

Consistent Team Training

Athletes train together as a unit throughout the program — building chemistry, trust, and the shared growth that only comes from consistent team training.

Is This the Right Fit?

Who Academy Is For

HVVC highly recommends the Academy for players and families who are interested in competing on a 12U–14U club volleyball team next season (approximate club season: November 2026 – April 2027).

If your athlete checks these boxes, Academy is a great fit:

  • ✓ Competitive and eager to improve
  • ✓ Enjoys being part of a team environment
  • ✓ Ready for fun, focused training
  • ✓ Has demonstrated interest and potential in volleyball
  • ✓ Looking to bridge from recreational play to club competition
Pathway to Club

Academy → Club Team

The Academy is intentionally designed as a bridge into HVVC's competitive club program. Athletes who complete the Academy enter fall tryouts with a significant advantage — they already know our coaching style, expectations, and culture.

This is your athlete's first step toward earning a spot on a 12U or 14U club team for the 2026–2027 season.
