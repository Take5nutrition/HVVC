What It Is

Build a Better Athlete

Great volleyball starts with a great athlete. HVVC's Speed & Performance program trains the physical foundation that makes everything else on the court possible — explosive first steps, efficient movement, and the strength to compete at full intensity from first to last point.

Led by Tatum Erickson, a Speed & Performance Coach and Nutrition Therapy Master who blends advanced movement science with functional nutrition to elevate athletic potential. Founder of Take5 Athletics and Take5 Nutrition.

This program is for HVVC athletes and open to athletes from any sport looking to improve their athletic foundation.

Training Pillars

What We Train

Sprint Mechanics

Proper acceleration, deceleration, and change-of-direction mechanics. Build the explosive speed that wins rallies.

Strength Fundamentals

Age-appropriate strength training that builds the foundation for power, stability, and long-term athletic development.

Movement Efficiency

Move better, waste less energy. Footwork patterns and body mechanics specific to volleyball court movement.

Injury Prevention

Targeted mobility, activation, and corrective work to keep athletes healthy and on the court all season long.

Sports Nutrition

Practical nutrition guidance from a certified Nutrition Therapy Master. Fuel, recover, and perform at your best.

Sport-Specific

Every drill and movement pattern is designed with volleyball demands in mind — vertical jump, lateral quickness, and court awareness.
