Training Programs

Ways to Train

From competitive club teams to flexible drop-in sessions - we offer year-round development for athletes at every level.

01

Club Teams

Professional coaching, structured practices, and competitive tournament play for athletes ready to compete at the next level.

  • Ages 10U–16U
  • 2x weekly practices
  • 2+ tournaments per month
  • Full uniform package
  • 12 players per roster
  • Power League Series

02

HVVC Academy

Focused, skill-based training without the full club commitment. Elite-level instruction in smaller group sessions designed to accelerate development.

  • All skill levels welcome
  • Smaller group sessions
  • Flexible commitment
  • Seasonal training blocks
Learn More →
03

Open Gyms

Refine your skills, connect with other players, and build confidence in a fun, high-energy environment. No commitment required.

  • All levels welcome
  • Drop-in friendly
  • No commitment required
  • Stay sharp off-season
Learn More →
04

Speed & Performance

Sprint mechanics, strength fundamentals, and movement efficiency to build faster, more resilient athletes on and off the court.

  • Sprint mechanics
  • Strength fundamentals
  • Sports nutrition guidance
  • Injury prevention
