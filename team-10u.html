Program Focus

Foundational skill development, team systems, positioning, court awareness, confidence, and communication. This program is designed to introduce young athletes to volleyball in a supportive, growth-oriented environment.

Development

What Athletes Learn

Foundational Skills

Passing, setting, serving, and hitting fundamentals taught through age-appropriate drills and games.

Team Systems

Introduction to basic rotations, positioning, and team play concepts to build court awareness.

Confidence & Communication

Building self-confidence, learning to communicate on the court, and developing a positive competitive mindset.

Court Awareness

Understanding spacing, movement patterns, and reading the game at an introductory level.

Culture

Team Expectations

Represent

Represent the club at all times, showing respect for yourself, your teammates, your coaches, and the organization.

Support

Support and encourage teammates on and off the court. Foster teamwork and sportsmanship in every situation.

Commit

Commit to regular attendance at practices. Consistent participation is key to skill development at this level.

Communication

GameChanger is used for tracking schedules and team announcements throughout the program.
