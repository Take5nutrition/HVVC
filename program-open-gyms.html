What It Is

Show Up. Play Ball.

HVVC Open Gyms are exactly what they sound like — open, energetic gym sessions where any athlete can come in, get reps, and enjoy the game. No roster spot required, no tryout, no commitment.

Whether you're staying sharp in the off-season, trying volleyball for the first time, or just want a fun competitive outlet, Open Gyms are the perfect low-pressure environment to grow your game and connect with the HVVC community.
