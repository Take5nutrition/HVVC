What It Is

Competitive Club Volleyball

HVVC Club Teams are the heart of everything we do. Built for athletes who want to compete — not just participate. Our club program runs from November through April with year-round development opportunities for those who want more.

Every team is coached by experienced staff committed to both the athlete's volleyball growth and personal development. We keep rosters small so every player gets real reps, real coaching, and a real shot at the court.

Program Details

What's Included

Practice Schedule

Two structured practices per week covering fundamentals, team systems, advanced skills, and conditioning.

Full Uniform Package

2 jerseys, 2 practice shirts, Nike spandex, knee pads, team sweatshirt, and a team ball bag.

Tournament Play

2+ tournaments per month plus Power League Series competition. Tournament registration is included in season fees.

Small Rosters

Max 12 players per roster. Every athlete gets significant court time and individualized coaching attention.

Expert Coaching

Coaches with club, high school, and college experience. Every coach is background checked and player-development focused.

Nonprofit Pricing

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we offer scholarships and payment plans. Finances should never keep an athlete off the court.
